Privacy Policy

What data do we collect?

This service records and retains a record of http requests as logged by our webserver. These records include the originating IP-address, a timestamp, the requested URL, the user-agent string, and the address of the previous web page from which a link to our service was followed. We have modified the logging-procedure of our webserver with the tool AnonIP (https://pypi.org/project/anonip/) such that the last 12 bits and the last 84 bits of IPv4 and IPv6 addresses, respectively, are masked in the logs and cannot be used to identify individual users or clients. These data are stored on a secured server within the Eawag network.

How will we use your data?

We use these data to track technical problems. We might derive anonymous summary statistics about the usage of our services from these data and publish them. Any such published statistic will be aggregated such that no inferences about individual clients or users can be drawn.

Cookies and tracking technology

This service does not use cookies. This service does not employ any other tracking technology and does not leak information to any third parties.
